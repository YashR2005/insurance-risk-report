## FSMY-001 | egress | Exit access and obstruction (Malaysia)
Means of egress shall at all times be maintained free of obstruction. Goods, pallets and stored materials shall not encroach upon designated escape routes or exit doors, and the clear width of exits and protected staircases shall be preserved to allow safe and unimpeded evacuation of occupants.
