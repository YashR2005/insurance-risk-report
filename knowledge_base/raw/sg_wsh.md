## WSSG-001 | machinery | Guarding of dangerous parts of machinery
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, Part on machinery — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Every dangerous part of machinery must be securely guarded unless it is in a position or of a construction as safe as a secure guard would provide. Guards must be of substantial construction and properly maintained, and machinery must be fitted with accessible emergency stop controls.

## WSSG-002 | work_at_height | Prevention of falls / work at height
source: WSH (Work at Heights) Regulations 2013; WSH (General Provisions) Regulations — https://www.mom.gov.sg/workplace-safety-and-health/work-at-heights
Where a person may fall from height, fall-prevention measures must be provided — guard-rails, barriers, covers or a travel-restraint/fall-arrest system anchored to an adequate point. Open sides, floor openings and edges must be protected, and a permit-to-work system applied to higher-risk work at height.

## WSSG-003 | hazardous_substances | Control and storage of hazardous substances
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, hazardous substances provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Hazardous substances must be controlled through labelling to the GHS, accessible Safety Data Sheets, and designated storage that segregates incompatible chemicals from each other and from ignition sources. Exposure must be controlled by ventilation and, where residual risk remains, suitable personal protective equipment that is provided, maintained and used.

## WSSG-004 | housekeeping | Safe access and housekeeping
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, safe access provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Floors, passageways and gangways must be kept free of obstruction and of substances likely to cause slips or trips, and a safe means of access to and egress from every workplace must be provided and maintained. Materials must be stacked and stored so as not to be liable to collapse.

## WSSG-005 | confined_space | Entry into confined spaces
source: WSH (Confined Spaces) Regulations 2009 — https://sso.agc.gov.sg
No person may enter a confined space unless the atmosphere has been tested and certified safe by a competent person, a confined-space entry permit has been issued, and adequate ventilation, isolation, communication and rescue arrangements are in place for the duration of the work.

## WSSG-006 | noise | Control of excessive noise
source: WSH (Noise) Regulations — https://sso.agc.gov.sg
Where workers are exposed to excessive noise (above the permissible exposure level of 85 dB(A) over 8 hours), the employer must reduce exposure through engineering controls, provide suitable hearing protection, demarcate noise hazard areas, and provide audiometric examinations for exposed workers.

## WSSG-007 | lighting_ventilation | Lighting and ventilation of workplaces
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, lighting and ventilation provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Every workplace and means of access must be provided with adequate and suitable lighting, and effective and suitable ventilation must be provided to keep workrooms supplied with fresh air and to render harmless, so far as practicable, fumes, dust and other impurities.

## WSSG-008 | risk_management | Workplace risk assessment
source: WSH (Risk Management) Regulations 2006 — https://sso.agc.gov.sg
The employer must conduct a risk assessment of the safety and health risks posed to any person by the work carried out, implement reasonably practicable control measures following the hierarchy of control, record the assessment, and review it regularly or after any incident.
