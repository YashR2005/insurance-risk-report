## MCSG-001 | lifting | Thorough examination of lifting equipment
source: WSH (Operation of Cranes) / lifting-equipment requirements; MOM Authorised Examiner regime — https://www.mom.gov.sg/workplace-safety-and-health/wsh-professionals/authorised-examiner-lifting-equipment
Every lifting appliance, lifting machine and lifting gear must be thoroughly examined by an Authorised Examiner at least once every 12 months, and no such equipment may be used unless a current certificate of test and examination has been issued. Examination covers visual inspection, load testing and functional testing of safety devices.

## MCSG-002 | pressure | Pressure vessel examination and certification
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, pressure vessel provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Pressure vessels and steam boilers must be examined and certified by an Authorised Examiner at the prescribed intervals, must not be operated above their certified safe working pressure, and must be fitted with maintained safety fittings such as relief valves and pressure gauges. A valid certificate of examination must be kept.

## MCSG-003 | machinery | Machinery maintenance and lock-out / tag-out
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, maintenance and isolation provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Machinery must be maintained in safe working order, and before cleaning, maintenance or clearing blockages the energy source must be isolated and locked out / tagged out to prevent inadvertent start-up. Only trained, authorised persons may carry out such work.

## MCSG-004 | scaffold | Scaffolds and elevated work platforms
source: WSH (Scaffolds) Regulations 2011 — https://sso.agc.gov.sg
Scaffolds must be designed, erected, altered and dismantled under the supervision of competent persons, inspected before use and at least once every seven days, and tagged to show their status. Mobile elevating work platforms must be operated only by trained, authorised operators.

## MCSG-005 | machinery | Use of dangerous machines by trained persons
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, dangerous machinery provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
Specified dangerous machines (such as power presses and woodworking machines) may be operated, cleaned or adjusted only by persons who have been properly trained and instructed, and the machine's guards and safety devices must be in position and functioning before use.

## MCSG-006 | pressure | Register of pressure vessels and boilers
source: WSH (General Provisions) Regulations, pressure vessel record provisions — https://sso.agc.gov.sg/SL/WSHA2006-RG1
The occupier must keep a register recording each steam boiler, steam receiver and air receiver, its safe working pressure and the dates and results of statutory examinations, and must make the register available for inspection. Equipment without a current examination record must be taken out of service.
